About Green Magic Resort

Nestled in the lush hills of Wayanad, Green Magic Resort offers a tranquil escape into nature — where luxury meets sustainability.

Our Story

Founded with the vision of creating a harmonious retreat in Kerala’s most scenic district, Green Magic Resort blends eco-friendly design with warm hospitality. We began as a small family-run getaway, and have grown into a resort that welcomes travelers from across the globe.

Our Philosophy

We believe in offering guests an authentic experience of Wayanad — through organic food, sustainable practices, and genuine cultural connections. Our cottages are built with natural materials, and our operations focus on minimizing environmental impact.

What Makes Us Unique

  • Panoramic views of the Western Ghats
  • Personalized service and curated nature experiences
  • Eco-conscious architecture and organic gardens
  • Proximity to waterfalls, wildlife sanctuaries, and spice plantations
